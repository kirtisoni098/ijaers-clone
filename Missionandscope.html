VISION, MISSION AND SCOPE OF THE JOURNAL

Vision:
To promote global research and applications of advanced technology for the development of society and to encourage the researchers to undertake the projects for the continuous development of human society and the nation. We are also committed to make the journal a center of knowledge around the world.

Mission:
To support and provide the platform to authors for high-quality research and development focus publications in the field of engineering and life science around the world. The journal is also committed to contributing the education at all levels of society by establishing an effective interface among the researchers with open access policy.

Scope:
IJAERS publishes original, peer-reviewed, engineering-based or Science research/ Review articles.

Sub-topics included:

All engineering fields i.e.: Computer, Electronic, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil Science; Medical field Challenges, Agriculture, Social Science, Education, Interdisciplinary, Management, Economics and Literature. For more topics, you can click here.



Frequency of Journal

This journal is a monthly peer reviewed journal with DOI (From CrossRef) and has 12 issues per Year.
